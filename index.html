Our Features

Powerful tools to protect your passwords with science-grade precision and control.

Military-grade Encryption

Your sensitive data deserves the strongest protection. ChemKey employs AES-256 encryption, the same standard trusted by governments and security experts worldwide. Your password vault is secured with a mathematical formula applied to Chemical knowledge so complex that it would take billions of years to crack with current technology, ensuring your digital keys remain truly private.



Password Generator

Say goodbye to weak, predictable passwords. ChemKey's built-in password generator creates unique, complex combinations of characters following the latest security recommendations. Customize length, include symbols, numbers, or create pronounceable options that balance security with memorability—all while ensuring each password is as unique as a molecular formula.



Local Storage Architecture

Unlike cloud-based alternatives, ChemKey keeps your password vault exclusively on your device. This fundamental design choice eliminates server breaches, man-in-the-middle attacks, and unwanted access by third parties. Your data truly remains in your hands, accessible even without an internet connection, giving you complete control of your digital identity, address, cards and passwords.



Password Health Analysis

ChemKey acts as your digital security lab, automatically identifying vulnerabilities in your password collection. The password health dashboard highlights duplicates, aging passwords, and those that don't meet modern security standards. Receive personalized recommendations to strengthen your overall security posture and protect against evolving threats.

󰯄

Secure Password Sharing

Sometimes you need to share access without compromising security. ChemKey's encrypted sharing feature lets you temporarily grant access to specific credentials without revealing the actual password. Control exactly how long the shared access lasts and revoke it at any time, maintaining the perfect balance between collaboration and security.

About Us

The team behind ChemKey and our mission to keep your digital identity secure.

Our Story

ChemKey was born from a simple observation: password security shouldn't depend on trusting third parties with your most sensitive information.

Started by a group of security-minded developers and cryptography enthusiasts, we set out to create a password manager that put control back in users' hands. Inspired by the precision and reliability of chemistry, we named our project ChemKey — where your digital keys are handled with scientific accuracy.

Today, we're proud to offer a completely free, open-source solution that never compromises on security or privacy. Our mission is to make robust security accessible to everyone, regardless of technical background or financial resources.

Privacy First

We believe your passwords should never leave your device without your explicit consent. All encryption happens locally, ensuring your data remains yours alone.

Open Source

Our code is completely transparent and available for review. We believe security through obscurity is no security at all. Trust should be verified, not just given.

Community Driven

ChemKey grows through community contributions and feedback. Our users are our collaborators in building a more secure digital world.
